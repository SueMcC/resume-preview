General Manager – esolutions (1999–2001): Commercialised the joint venture between EDS, Microsoft, and Telecom NZ to deliver integrated cloud-based IT services. Managed strategic alliances, regulatory relationships, and commercial delivery in a first-of-its-kind enterprise technology environment.

Early Career – EDS, Netway Communications, Telecom NZ (1987–1999): Held progressive leadership roles spanning service design, business analysis, product development, and marketing in the IT&T industry. Built a strong foundation in data-driven service management, performance reporting, and cross-functional delivery.
